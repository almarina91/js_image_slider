Barcelona chair

Ludwig Mies van der Rohe

1929

The Rolex and Rolls-Royce of 20th-century chairs. Designed to complement his German Pavilion for the 1929 International Exhibition in Barcelona, the chair was never intended to be mass-produced. It was intended to just sit there looking fabulous at the Pavilion. It did. It still does. It's not all that comfortable, but it looks as stunning as any chair ever.
